Why Rabindranath Tagore University?

Rabindranath Tagore University Bhopal welcomes you with fifteen magnificent buildings in an entirely green campus located between two hills. The panoramic view of the university and the design of the campus are inspired from the ancient universities like Taxshila, Nalanda, and Shantiniketan. The RNTU also hosts world class infrastructure with experienced faculty, and a respectable placement record. Rabindranath Tagore University is India’s first skill-based university with a special focus on practical and research-based learning. Some of the other benefits you might find here are:

National Outlook-

RNTU is the leading part of the AISECT group of Universities with 5 universities situates in the states of Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Bihar, and Jharkhand that aim at increasing the accessibility and affordability of higher education. The aim is also to provide high quality higher education through skill development, research, entrepreneurial motivation and overall growth and development of the personalities.

Core Research Group (CRG)-

RNTU has established a Core Research Group (CRG) with an allocation of Rs. 1 Crore seed fund and an annual Rs. 1 Crore to promote search.

Research & Academic Collaborations-

RNTU organizes and takes part in 30 national and 15 international level collaborations, one mega international/conference/national seminar along with several research/academic workshop events every year.

UGC Approved Journals-

The university’s in-house publication has published two UGC-approved research journals in the last 6 years that are available online and are also indexed by Copernicus.

Scholarship:

The university provides financial assistance with the Shiksha Mitra Scholarship to aid the meritorious students from backward parts of the country and those with a financially weak background. The scholarship is provided to help students in their studies without worrying about the fee and cost of books. The university also provides financial assistance to the faculty so that they can upgrade the qualification.

Environment & Resources:

The university provides various facilities to the students like faculty with Doctorates and industry background, ICT loaded industry based CBCS curriculum, state of the art laboratories with industry grade equipment, etc.

Project Unnat Bharat:

The project was awarded to the university by Ministry of Human Resource and Development (MHRD). The university is currently working for the upliftment of 5 villages all the while providing holistic training for every student.

IoT & Cloud Computing Lab:

Rabindranath Tagore University is the first university in Madhya Pradesh to establish IoT (Internet of Things) laboratory by Frugal and Intel and Cloud Computing Lab by Microsoft.

Creative Environment:

Along with various cultural and co-curricular activities that are organized all the time, the university offers excellent facilities, motivation, support, and reward to the faculty and students who have published over 1000 papers and 50 books in a short span of just 5 years.

Navaratn Centres of Excellence:

The university has 9 centres of excellence Material Science, Energy, Earth & Space Science, Arts & Literature, Agriculture, IoT and Computing, Entrepreneurship, Science Communication, and Environmental Science.

Skill Academies:

Established to provide skill-based education to the Indian citizenry, RNTU has established 7 skill academies to impart skill-based training in 55 skills.

Education Portal:

Rabindranath Tagore University has made all its services and information available to the general public online through the M.P. Education Portal.

World Class Laboratories:

RNTU provides its students with state-of-the-art well-equipped laboratories and equipment to help the ascertain their abilities in the practical application of the theory taught in various subjects.

Campus Life:

The university offers excellent hostel facility, sports facilities of global standard, canteen, clubs for debate, drama, and literature, and also organises various cultural events and competitions throughout the year.

Extension Work:

The university continuously works to aid various government policies and plans like MPOnline, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Swach Bharat, Digital India, Jandhan Yojana, etc.
